About The Bromsgrove Herald

Independent local journalism serving Bromsgrove and the surrounding district since 1858.

Our Story

The Bromsgrove Herald has been at the heart of local life in Bromsgrove and the surrounding villages for more than 160 years. Founded in 1858 as a weekly broadsheet, the Herald has grown into one of the most trusted sources of news, sport and community information across the district.

We cover the stories that matter to local people — from planning decisions and council business to school news, sport and the issues affecting everyday life across Bromsgrove, Redditch, Alvechurch, Rubery, Catshill, Barnt Green and the wider north Worcestershire area.

Our Journalism

We believe in straightforward, accurate, independent reporting. Our journalists are based in the communities they cover and are committed to holding those in authority to account while giving a voice to the people who make Bromsgrove what it is.

We take our responsibility to our readers seriously. We correct errors promptly, we protect our sources, and we do not allow commercial relationships to influence our editorial decisions.
